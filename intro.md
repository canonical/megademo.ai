Hey everyone,

With the Madrid Engineering sprint starting in just over three weeks, there is no better time to highlight an activity we have been designing with the engineering leads, which is a follow-up from multiple teams' requests to organize a specialized hacking session.

That’s right, we will be hosting a hackathon during the sprint, centered around AI in the context of engineering. As many of you already experienced (and hopefully experimented), every engineer suddenly got access to superpowers - and it’s time to properly exercise our new magical abilities. 

While we are finalizing the details (more to come early next week), the general principles are as follows:

###  **The Schedule**

* **Workshop and intro kickoff:** On Monday, we will have an intro session, which is currently being finalized as we speak by @gschiano  and Matias, focusing on agentic engineering best practices

* **Compo (Hackathon):** Wednesday, 09:00 – 18:00. We will be using the team’s space to reduce disruption as much as possible

* **Megademo on Friday:** demostyle inspired (obviously) Megademo highlighting the best compos (again, more details to come)

### **The Rules of “the Scene”**

* **AI x Domain Innovation:** While the theme is AI, your project *must* relate to your product/team or subject matter/specialisation. 

* **The challenge**: Many of us are already used to working with AI agents on assigned tasks. The true test here will be to collaborate and coordinate with other engineers in a limited timeframe while leveraging AI in agentic form to deliver a common demo

* **Shared experience:** we aim at having groups share their best practice, tooling, and ways of working

* **Outcome:** by the end of the hackathon, we expect teams to provide a link to a repository containing the demo and its source (no commit after this) and a couple of minutes recording session

* **Structure**: we aim at having manageable groups within each product/team expecting everyone to get a hands-on experience on this

* **Target audience:** all teams in engineering must contribute. That’s right, **fun is mandatory** :)

### **💾 The Megademo**

To keep the overall process manageable, we will be working with all leads (your VP or Director of Engineering) to work on a good selection process for the top entry from their area. Again, more details coming in the next few days.

There’s no time as of today, and it’s time to surf this unique wave of changes, once a lifetime transformation that surrounds us. And we believe we are taking the wave “just at the right time”...
